Content Policy – AIO Video Downloader

Last Updated: July 31, 2025


AIO Video Downloader is an open-source tool designed for downloading publicly available videos while prioritizing user privacy, simplicity, and ethical usage. By using this app, you agree to comply with the following policy:


1. Respect Copyright and Ownership Rights

  • No Hosting or Storage: The app does not host, store, or distribute any media files. It functions solely as a tool to access publicly available content.
  • Legal Responsibility: Users must ensure that downloaded content complies with:
    • Local and international copyright laws (e.g., DMCA, EU Copyright Directive).
    • The terms of the source platform (e.g., YouTube’s Terms of Service).
  • Prohibited Downloads: Avoid downloading if:
    • You lack explicit permission from the copyright holder.
    • The content is behind a paywall, subscription, or marked as private.
    • The source platform explicitly prohibits downloads (e.g., Netflix, Disney+).
  • Fair Use: Limited downloads for personal, educational, or transformative use (e.g., criticism, commentary) may be permissible under fair use doctrines, but legal advice is recommended.

2. No Piracy or Illegal Use

  • Strictly Forbidden: The app must not be used for:
    • Downloading pirated movies, TV shows, or premium content without authorization.
    • Circumventing DRM (Digital Rights Management) protections.
    • Accessing region-locked or licensed content unlawfully.
  • Examples of Violations:
    • Downloading a copyrighted film from a streaming service without a license.
    • Distributing downloaded content via torrents or file-sharing platforms.
  • Legal Consequences: Violators may face penalties under laws like the DMCA or GDPR.

3. Respect Platform Terms

  • Compliance Required: Users must adhere to the Terms of Service (ToS) of the source platform (e.g., YouTube’s prohibition on automated downloads).
  • Platform-Specific Rules:
    • YouTube: Allows downloads only via its official "Offline" feature or with creator consent.
    • Instagram/Facebook: Prohibits scraping or downloading without permission.
  • Account Suspension Risk: Violating platform ToS may result in bans or legal action by the platform.

4. No Re-Uploading or Redistribution

  • Personal Use Only: Downloaded content is strictly for offline viewing unless explicit permission is granted by the copyright holder.
  • Prohibited Actions:
    • Re-uploading to social media, video platforms, or commercial websites.
    • Modifying and redistributing content without attribution (e.g., memes, edits).
    • Selling or monetizing downloaded files.
  • Creator Rights: Always credit original creators and respect Creative Commons licenses (e.g., CC BY-NC).

5. Privacy and Data

  • No Data Collection: The app does not:
    • Store, transmit, or log user activity (e.g., downloaded URLs, IP addresses).
    • Require accounts, permissions, or network access beyond fetching public videos.
  • Local Processing: All operations occur on your device; no third-party servers are involved.
  • Transparency: The open-source nature of the app allows independent verification of privacy claims.

6. User Responsibility

  • Liability: Users assume all legal risks associated with downloads. The developers disclaim responsibility for misuse.
  • Due Diligence: Verify content legality using tools like:
    • Copyright databases (e.g., U.S. Copyright Office).
    • Platform-specific policies (e.g., YouTube’s "License" metadata).
  • Ethical Use: Prioritize downloading from creators who explicitly permit it (e.g., via CC licenses).

7. Reporting and Enforcement

  • Takedown Requests: Copyright holders may report violations via:
    • Email: shiba.spj@hotmail.com
    • GitHub: https://github.com/shibaFoss/AIO
  • Action Taken: Valid complaints will result in:
    • Removal of infringing content links from the app’s supported sources.
    • Potential termination of access for repeat offenders.

⚠️ Disclaimer: This policy may be updated without notice. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the latest terms. Consult legal counsel for specific compliance questions.
